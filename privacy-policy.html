Privacy Policy (Milk Dairy Hisab)

Hum aapki privacy ka poora sammaan karte hain. Humara app "Milk Dairy Hisab" ek bahut hi simple tool hai.

1. Koi Login Zaroori Nahi

Humare app ko chalane ke liye aapko Mobile Number, Email, ya OTP ki koi zaroorat nahi hai. App kholte hi aap seedha apna kaam shuru kar sakte hain.

2. Koi Permission Nahi

Humara app aapke phone se koi bhi personal permission (jaise Camera, Contacts, ya Location) nahi maangta.

3. Internet ka Istemal

Dhyan dein: Humara app sirf tabhi kaam karta hai jab aapka Internet (Data) On rehta hai. Ye sirf app ki files load karne ke liye internet use karta hai, aapka koi personal data collect nahi karta.

4. Data Storage

Aap jo bhi hisab-kitab app mein save karte hain, wo aapke phone ki browser storage mein hi rehta hai.

Agar aapka koi sawal hai, toh aap humein contact kar sakte hain.
